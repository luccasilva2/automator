Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Repost Automator collects and uses information.

1. Data Collected

We collect only the minimum information required to authenticate and authorize your TikTok account using the official API.

2. Authentication Tokens

OAuth tokens are stored securely and used exclusively to upload videos to TikTok on your behalf.

3. No Data Sharing

Your data is not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

4. User Rights

You can revoke access at any time through TikTok's account settings.

5. Security

We apply security practices to protect authentication data and uploaded content.

6. Content Usage

Videos handled through this service are only uploaded to accounts you authorize.
